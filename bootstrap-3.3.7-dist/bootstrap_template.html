Census and Voter Lists

These records often include details to help pinpoint someone's location at a particular point in time. While the census records vary from place to place, and year to year, you can find information like names of other household members, ages, birthplaces, residence, occupation, immigration and citizenship details, marriage information, military service and more.

Birth, Marriage and Death Collections

These records, collectively known as vital records, can provide details about important milestones in your ancestors’ lives. They include information like the event date and place, parents’ names, occupation and residence. The cause of death is also included in most death records.

Immigration and Travel

The records in this collection include passenger arrival records, naturalization records, border crossings, emigration records, passports, convict transportation records. The collection includes immigration records from the U.S. and several other countries from around the world.

Military

Military records are wonderful sources that provide unique facts and insights into the lives of men and women who have served in the armed forces. The types of records you’ll find in this category include draft records, service records, pension records, bounty land records, claim records, and military histories.

Schools, Directories and Church Histories

These records are helpful in placing your ancestor in a specific location in a particular year. Other collections in this category include church histories, church records, alumni lists, yearbooks, professional directories, and phone books.

Tax, Criminal, Land and Wills

Court, land, wills, and financial records can be incredibly rich in detail due to the fact that family details and witnesses were often used to identify people. In this category, you’ll find indexes as well as some collections of actual records.
